Sitka Salmon Game

Who we are: CS seniors Mila, Steven, and Wyatt

Our Project: We worked with the Sitka Sound Science Center to make an educational video game for use in their aquarium/gift shop. This game covers the salmon life cycle in Sitka, Alaska from an egg to a spawning salmon.
